Foot and Mouth Disease (FMD) Global Strategy

The overall objective of the Global Strategy is to improve animal production, food security and economic opportunities, particularly in developing countries, and thereby alleviate poverty, increase income generation and improve the livelihoods of small farmers and general human wellbeing. The objective of the Global Strategy is also to maintain the production and export capacities and the status of the countries free from FMD.
